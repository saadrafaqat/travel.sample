Bali: Soul & Sea

📍 Indonesia

About This Journey

Embark on a transformative 10-day exploration through Bali's most sacred and stunning landscapes. From the terraced rice fields of Ubud to the pristine beaches of Uluwatu, this journey weaves together culture, spirituality, and natural beauty.

Each day is thoughtfully crafted to balance adventure with serenity. Practice yoga at sunrise, explore hidden waterfalls, participate in traditional ceremonies, and discover the island's culinary treasures with local chefs.

Day-by-Day Itinerary

01

Arrival in Ubud

Welcome dinner at a traditional Balinese compound. Settle into your private villa nestled among rice paddies.

02

Tegallalang & Sacred Waters

Explore the iconic rice terraces. Afternoon purification ceremony at Tirta Empul temple.

03

Hidden Waterfalls Trek

Hike through lush jungle to discover three secret waterfalls. Picnic lunch by cascading waters.

04

Artisan Village & Cooking Class

Visit silver and woodcarving workshops. Learn to cook authentic Balinese dishes.

05

Mount Batur Sunrise

Pre-dawn trek to witness sunrise from the volcanic rim. Hot springs recovery afterwards.
